      The Photographic Studio

      Welcome to the Photographic Studio in Room 203 at Grand Parade. The studio can be booked via the Photo Service Unit Team by dropping in to see us (Photo Unit Room 241 at Grand Parade)

      Emailing us at: photographicserviceunit@brighton.ac.uk.

      The studio can be booked for morning sessions 9.30-1.00, afternoon session 2.00-5.00 (2.00-4.00 on a Friday as we close an hour earlier), or if necessary you can book an all day session. Please book you studio slot early (at least a week in advance, maybe 2-3 when deadlines approach) as we get very busy.
