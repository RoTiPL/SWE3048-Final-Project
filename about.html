      The Photographic Studio

      Welcome to the Photographic Studio in Room 203 at Grand Parade. The studio can be booked via the Photo Service Unit Team by dropping in to see us (Photo Unit Room 241 at Grand Parade)

      Emailing us at: photographicserviceunit@brighton.ac.uk.

      The studio can be booked for morning sessions 9.30-1.00, afternoon session 2.00-5.00 (2.00-4.00 on a Friday as we close an hour earlier), or if necessary you can book an all day session. Please book you studio slot early (at least a week in advance, maybe 2-3 when deadlines approach) as we get very busy.

      Safety in the Studio

    • Always inform a Technician of the work you are undertaking before starting in the studio so we can best support you and ensure you stay safe.
    • No food or drink is to be consumed in the studio.
    • Report all faulty equipment and breakages to a Technician as soon as possible.
    • Take care of trailing leads and the lights which can become very hot.

      Camera Lenses

      AF-S Nikkor 24-120mm F2.8D
      AF-S Nikkor 50mm F1.4
      AF Nikkor 35-70mmn F2.8

      Each camera has variable lenses available depending on what your are shooting. A standard lens is 50mm which is the nearest to how the eye sees. Please let us know when you book the studio what you are planning to do so we can book the correct lens for you i.e. close-up work, wide angle etc.
